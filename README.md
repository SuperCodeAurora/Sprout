## The Concept
**Sprout** is a TUI (Terminal User Interface) companion designed to weaponize your **empathy** against your procrastination.

It doesn't just track your work; it *feels* your work habits. It lives in your shell, watching your progress.

* **1 Task = 1 Coin (Nutrient).**
* **0 Tasks = Starvation.**
